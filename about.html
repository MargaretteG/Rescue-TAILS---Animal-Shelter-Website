ABOUT US

Rescue TAILS: Taking Animals Into Loved & Sheltered was founded with a simple yet powerful goal: to provide loving homes for animals in need and to promote the importance of adoption over purchasing pets. We believe that every animal, regardless of their past, deserves a second chance at life, filled with love, care, and security. Through our efforts, we aim to reduce pet homelessness, raise awareness about responsible pet ownership, and connect compassionate adopters with animals who are eager to find their forever homes.

OUR MISSION

We rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned, abused, and neglected animals. We strive to create a safe space for them to heal and thrive, offering each animal the love and care they deserve. By promoting adoption, we aim to decrease the number of animals in shelters and reduce the cycle of overpopulation.

OUR VISION

A world where every animal has a loving home and where pet homelessness is a thing of the past. Through community engagement, education, and adoption initiatives, we aspire to foster a society that values compassion and care for all animals.

Rescue, Rehome, Rejoice


Our purpose is to give animals a second chance at life. Rescue TAILS is a platform dedicated to showcasing the animals in our care, raising awareness about the joys and responsibilities of pet adoption, and making it easy for individuals and families to find their perfect pet. Through Rescue Tails, we not only save animals but also inspire a culture of kindness and responsible pet ownership.
