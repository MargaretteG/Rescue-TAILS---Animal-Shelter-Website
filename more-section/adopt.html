ADOPT A PET

Find Your Perfect Companion: Adopt a Friend for Life!


At Rescue Tails, we believe every animal deserves a loving home. This is your chance to change a life and bring joy into your own. Explore our incredible selection of cats and dogs waiting for their forever families. With just a few simple steps, you can turn your dream of pet companionship into reality. Let’s embark on this journey together and find the perfect match for you!

3-Steps Adoption Process:

Visit our Shelter

Explore Rescue TAILS and discover the wide variety of dogs and cats eager to find a loving home. Each visit brings you closer to meeting a furry friend.

Visit your Future Pet

Get to know your future pet by spending quality time together. Allow yourself to form a connection and ensure they’ll be the right fit for your home.

Take your Pet Home

After finding your perfect match, you can now bring your new pet home. This is the moment where a new chapter begins!

Step 1: Visit our Shelter
  • Start your adoption journey by visiting the Rescue Tails shelter. Don’t forget to bring a valid ID.
  • Fill out the adoption form to begin the process.
  • Participate in a short interview to help us match you with the right pet.
  • Meet our amazing pets in person, each one eager for a loving home.
  • Wait for the vet's clearance and set a pick-up date for your new furry friend.
Step 2: Visit your Future Pet
  • Return for another visit to spend more time with your chosen pet and confirm your bond.
  • (Optional) Bring along children or other pets from your household to ensure everyone gets along.
  • Don’t forget to bring a leash and collar, or a carrier if you’re adopting a cat or a small dog.
  • Finalize your adoption by paying the fee: ₱500 for cats or ₱1,000 for dogs.
Step 3: Take your Pet Home
  • It’s time for the best part—bringing your new family member home! Ensure your pet is comfortable and loved as they settle into their forever home.
  • “The journey to adoption is rewarding—this is the start of a lifetime of love, care, and companionship.”

Your New Best Friend is Waiting!


By choosing to adopt, you’re not only giving a pet a second chance at life, but you're also gaining a loyal companion who will bring endless joy and love into your home. Every adoption saves lives—open your heart, take the first step, and make a difference today.
