Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
  1. What is Rescue TAILS?
    • Rescue Tails is a dedicated shelter for rescuing animals in need, raising awareness about pet adoption, and connecting individuals and families with their perfect pets. We strive to provide a loving environment for animals awaiting adoption.
  2. How can I adopt a pet?
      To adopt a pet, follow these simple steps:
    • Visit our shelter to meet the animals.
    • Fill out the adoption application form.
    • Participate in an interview with our staff.
    • Once approved, you can take your new pet home!
  3. What are the adoption fees?
      Our adoption fees are as follows:
    • Cats: 500 pesos
    • Dogs: 1000 pesos
    • These fees help cover the costs of veterinary care, vaccinations, and other expenses associated with rescuing and caring for our animals.
  4. Can I visit the shelter before adopting?
    • Absolutely! We encourage potential adopters to visit our shelter to meet our animals and learn more about our adoption process.
  5. What should I bring when I visit the shelter?
    • When visiting our shelter, please bring a valid ID. If you plan to adopt, consider bringing a leash and collar for dogs or a carrier for cats.
  6. Are the pets at Rescue TAILS vaccinated and spayed/neutered?
    • Yes! All our animals are up-to-date on vaccinations and are spayed or neutered before adoption to ensure their health and well-being.
  7. Can I adopt if I have other pets at home?
    • Yes! We encourage families with pets to adopt. We recommend bringing your current pets to meet the new pet to ensure compatibility. Our staff can also provide guidance on how to introduce new pets to your home.
  8. What if I can’t keep the pet I adopt?
    • We understand that circumstances may change. If you cannot keep your adopted pet, please contact us. We will work with you to find a suitable solution and may take the pet back into our care.
  9. How can I help if I can’t adopt?
    • There are many ways to support Rescue TAILS! You can volunteer your time, donate supplies or funds, foster animals in need, or help spread the word about our mission and available pets.
  10. Where can I find updates on available pets?
    • You can find information about our available pets on our website.
